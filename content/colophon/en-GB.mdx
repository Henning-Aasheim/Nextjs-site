What exactly is a colophon? To be quite honest, I didn't know either until I began looking at other websites for how they were built. A colophon comes from the Greek word <Lang lang='el'>κολοφών</Lang> (kolofoon), meaning *summit* or *top*, metaphorically used to mean to *finish* or *complete*. The word itself is connected to the Greek word for *hill*: <Lang lang='el'>κολώνη</Lang> (koloonee).
</DropCap>

Colophon today is usually defined as a small text describing the production of a book, newspaper, etc., and here I will use the colophon to document how I made this page; the technical details, as well as the different considerations I had to make.
